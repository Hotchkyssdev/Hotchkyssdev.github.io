TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

Design and Construction


The cooling tower is designed with a high-quality PVC pipe structure. endurance. The tower body is covered with insulating material so that it can thermally insulate the water. The main components include PVC pipes inside, a sponge filling material plant and an air motor to enter the tower.

Cooling Process


Hot water enters the top of the tower and flows through the filling material (Vegetable Sponge), which increases the contact surface between the water and the air. The air motor generates an upward current of air that passes through the filling, causing the water to evaporate. This evaporation process absorbs heat from the remaining water and the surrounding air, thus achieving temperature reduction.

Results of Our Tests


During two trials, promising results were observed, highlighting the tower's cooling efficiency and capacity. In the first trial, using water at 65 degrees Celsius, the temperature dropped to approximately 35 degrees Celsius as it passed through the tower, demonstrating a reduction in temperature and the system's effectiveness in extracting heat. In the second trial, using water at 80 degrees Celsius, a decrease to approximately 40 degrees Celsius was observed, confirming the tower's effectiveness in cooling water, even under more challenging conditions.
