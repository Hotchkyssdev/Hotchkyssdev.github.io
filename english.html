WHAT IS AQUAFENIX?

¿What is AquaFenix?


AquaFenix ​​is ​​a company formed with knowledge of Web Development, Mobile Development and Thermodynamics.

¿Why the name AquaFenix?


We chose the name AquaFenix ​​because of the water and the aquamarine color "Aqua" and "Fenix" because of the resurgence like a phoenix after previous frustrated projects by our members.

¿What we offer?


Our company offers a product used for cooling water in industrial or domestic contexts. With the product we manufacture we achieve a reduction in temperature, which makes this product ideal for different environments of use, having the capacity to be used either in factories or in the kitchen of your home. All this happens thanks to the fact that the cooling system of the device performs a temperature exchange between the hot water and the cold air, which produces a decrease in temperature in the water.

¿What is our mission?


Our mission as a company is to provide efficient and safe water cooling solutions for various daily needs of companies and individuals. We are committed to improving quality of life and optimizing operational processes through high-performance water cooling systems.
